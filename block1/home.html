Lose Weight

If an individual wants to lose weight he/she must follow a calorie stricted diet(This doesn't mean to starve but to eat less). Typically a person daily calorie intake must lie between 1500-1800 calories per day coming from protein,carbohydrates and fats, carbohydates intake must be low and boiled vegetables should be included instead of this and healthy fats eg almonds,peanut butter can be increased and Water intake must be 5-6 litres per day

Gain Weight

For all those skinny underweight people putting on weight has been a big problem for years, resulting in weakness in their body inorder to gain weight and stay healthy so the diet should be calorie surplus ie it should be in the range of 2400-3000 calories per day

Diet

Protein sources : Egg,Chicken,Fish ( for non-veg) Tofu,Paneer,Soyabean,Sprouts,Dals (for veg) Carbohydates sources: Brown rice, Oatmeals,Brown bread, Green veggies Fats sources: Walnuts,almonds,peanut butter.

Exercises

    Cardiovascular exercise

    • Jogging (20-30 mins)
    • Skipping (100-200 skips)
    • Swimming(20-30 mins)

    Weight Training

    • Arms exercises ie. Dumbell curls,tricep dips
    • Chest exercises ie. Pushups,Benchpress
    • Abs exercises ie. Crunches,planks
